The Island's Legacy: Golden Mouse

Centuries ago, a notorious pirate named Captain Blackbeard roamed the seas, plundering ships and amassing a vast treasure trove. He was feared by sailors and loved by his crew, who followed him to the ends of the earth in search of wealth and adventure.

One day, Captain Blackbeard and his crew stumbled upon a deserted island in the middle of the ocean. They decided to bury their treasure on the island, thinking it would be safe from prying eyes. However, the island was cursed by a powerful sorceress who vowed to protect the treasure with her

Over the years, many adventurers tried to find the treasure, but none succeeded. Legend has it that the sorceress placed a series of clues and traps on the island to prevent anyone from claiming the treasure.

Now, it's your turn to try your luck. You are an adventurer who has heard the legend of Captain Blackbeard's treasure and has decided to set sail for the cursed island. You must use your wits and skills to solve the clues, disarm the traps, and claim the treasure before the island is engulfed by a deadly storm.
