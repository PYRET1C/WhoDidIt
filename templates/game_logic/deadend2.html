As soon as you open the doors of the treasure room you find out it is completely empty. Did someone found the treasure before you?? You look around the room and you find an old piece of paper that says:

"Ahoy there, ye scurvy dogs! If ye be reading this, then ye have found my so-called 'treasure.' But let me tell ye, I made a fool of ye all! There be no treasure here, no gold, no jewels, nothin' but an empty room. And even if there were, do ye really think I'd make it so easy for ye to find? Nay, I'm no fool. But don't be too disappointed, me hearties. Ye may have not found treasure, but ye did find adventure. And isn't that what ye really came for? Now go on, get outta here before I change me mind and send me crew after ye!"

As you read the note, you can feel your heart sink. The words on the page reveal the truth you were dreading: there is no treasure here. The pirate king had played you all for fools, leading you on a wild goose chase with a map that was never meant to lead to any riches.

You can't help but feel a sense of disappointment and anger at the same time. After all the trials and obstacles you've faced, it seems like all your efforts were for nothing. But then again, you can't help but admire the pirate king's cunning and deception. If there really was any treasure to be found, he wouldn't have made it so easy to locate.

You take a deep breath and steel yourself for the journey back. It's not the outcome you were hoping for, but at least you still have your wits and your life. And who knows? Maybe there's another adventure waiting just around the corner...

As you descend from the treasure room, you couldn't shake off the feeling that there might be more to discover in this castle. You decided to check out the dungeons, thinking that there might be something hidden there.
